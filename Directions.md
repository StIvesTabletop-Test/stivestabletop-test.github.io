# Public Transport

## Buses

Stagecoach services A, B and D stop close to the Corn Exchange at St Ives Station Road (first map - in the direction of Cambridge)/St Ives Bus Station (second map - in the direction of Huntingdon/Peterborough) and St Ives Park & Ride (see Parking - both directions). The timetable can be found [here](https://www.thebusway.info/routes-times.shtml).

In the early evening (in time for the start of sessions), services arrive at these stops from both Cambridge and Huntingdon/Peterborough around every 10 minutes.

The last service in the direction of Cambridge on Wednesday evenings leave St Ives Station Road at 22:40 (B).

The last services in the direction of Huntingdon/Peterborough on Wednesday evenings leave St Ives Bus Station at 23:03 (B) and 00:03 (B).

Other local bus services serve St Ives Bus Station.
